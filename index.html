Welcome to Synonyms

How To Play

Synonyms are words that have nearly the same meaning. Like when someone says "PE class made me exhausted." and another student says "PE class made me tired." We know they both have the same attitude to PE class. To play choose a partner, then click start below or choose your own words with the yellow button. You and your partner will take turns answering until the winner reaches 5 correct answers! Good luck!
